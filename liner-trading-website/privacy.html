What We Collect

Request for Quote and Contact forms: the company name, contact name, email, phone, and order details you type in. These forms open your own email client with the message pre-filled (via a mailto: link) — by default, that information goes directly to our inbox and isn't stored on any server we run. If we've connected these forms to a CRM, that system receives the same data under its own privacy terms.

Client Portal accounts: your email and password (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase — we never see your raw password) and any order records our team links to your account. Logging in stores a session token in your browser's local storage so you stay logged in between visits.

The AI trade assistant: messages you type into the chat widget are sent to our AI provider (xAI) to generate a response. If you're logged in when you use it, your own order number, product, and shipment stage may be included as context so the assistant can answer questions about your order — never another customer's data. We don't store a transcript of these conversations on our own servers; they reset when you reload the page.

We do not currently use analytics or advertising cookies.

Who We Share It With

Supabase hosts our authentication and order-tracking database. xAI processes AI assistant conversations. Airtable holds our product catalog only — no customer or order data is ever sent there. We don't sell personal data to anyone.

Your Choices

To access, correct, or delete the data linked to your account, contact us at the address below. Logging out clears your local session; deleting your account removes your login credentials from our authentication provider.

Children's Privacy

This is a business-to-business trade site and isn't directed at, or knowingly used by, children.

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page if what we collect or share changes. Check back periodically.

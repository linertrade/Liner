Division 03 · Import Trading

Sourcing Into Ethiopia

This is a separate trade direction from the export catalog: goods coming into Ethiopia, not out of it — so it runs on its own page, not folded into the export RFQ engine.

What We Import

Plastics & Plastic Articles

Liner Trading PLC sources plastics and plastic articles from overseas suppliers and brings them into the Ethiopian market, using the same customs clearance and trade-finance relationships that support the export side of the business.

Exact sub-categories and specifications vary by shipment — since this line runs opposite to the export catalog (Ethiopia is the destination, not the origin), the Incoterm/destination-port structure of the RFQ form doesn't apply here. Tell us what you're sourcing and where it's coming from, and we'll follow up directly.

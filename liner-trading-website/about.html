About the Company

Where Trade Meets Trust

Liner Trading PLC has operated out of Addis Ababa since 2015, sourcing and exporting Ethiopian agricultural commodities, manufacturing iodized salt, and importing plastics for the domestic market.

Profile

A Trading House Built on Three Ledgers

Liner Trading PLC is a private Ethiopian trading company headquartered on Ethio-China Street in Addis Ababa. The company sources and exports pulses, oilseeds and coffee to buyers abroad, manufactures iodized salt domestically, and imports plastics and plastic articles into the Ethiopian market.

Quality control and reliable turnaround sit at the center of how the company operates — from sourcing through export documentation to delivery at the destination port.

How the Business Breaks Down

Agricultural Export

The core of the business. Chickpeas, kidney beans, haricot beans, mung beans, lentils, faba beans, field peas, sesame seeds and green coffee — sold by the container to buyers across India, Pakistan, Turkey and beyond.

View export catalog →

Salt Manufacturing

Under a Memorandum of Understanding with the Ethiopian Investment Commission, Liner Trading leases a processing shed at Semera Industrial Park in the Afar Region to produce iodized salt for the domestic market.

Semera Industrial Park · Afar Region

Import Trading

Plastics and plastic articles are sourced from overseas suppliers and brought into the Ethiopian market, using the same customs and logistics relationships that support the export business.

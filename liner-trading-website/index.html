What We Do

Three Lines, One Ledger

Agricultural export is the core of the business. Two more divisions run alongside it.

Division 01

Agricultural Export

Pulses (chickpeas, kidney beans, haricot beans, mung beans, lentils, faba beans, field peas), sesame seeds and green coffee, sold by the container to buyers worldwide.

Division 02

Salt Manufacturing

Iodized salt production under an MOU with the Ethiopian Investment Commission, at a processing shed in Semera Industrial Park, Afar Region.

Division 03

Import Trading

Sourcing plastics and plastic articles into the Ethiopian market, backed by the same trade-finance and customs relationships as the export side.

Work It Out Before You Ask

Two calculators that usually take a phone call: how much container space you need, and who's responsible for what once goods leave the warehouse.

Tool 01

CBM / Container Calculator

Enter your carton dimensions and quantity. If you know the per-carton weight, add it too — dense cargo often hits the weight limit before the space runs out.

Rule-of-thumb capacities used here
ContainerVolumeWeight (road limit)
20ft Standard~28 CBM~28,000 kg
40ft Standard~58 CBM~28,000 kg
40ft High Cube~68 CBM~28,000 kg

Note the weight limit barely changes between sizes — road transport, not the container itself, usually caps payload. That's why dense cargo (like salt) can max out a 20ft long before it's full.
